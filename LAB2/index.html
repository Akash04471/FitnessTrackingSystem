What is Fitness Tracking?

Fitness tracking refers to the use of technology to monitor and record fitness-related metrics such as distance walked or run, calorie consumption, and in some cases heartbeat and quality of sleep. Devices such as smartphones, smartwatches, and wearable fitness trackers have revolutionized the way people manage their health and wellbeing.

  • Set and track your fitness goals effectively
  • Monitor your daily activity and health stats
  • Get real-time feedback on your performance
  • Stay motivated with achievement badges and community challenges
